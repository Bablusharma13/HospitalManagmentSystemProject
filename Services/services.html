Our Services

At City General Hospital, we offer a wide range of medical services to meet the healthcare needs of our community. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing the highest quality care.

Emergency Care

Our emergency department is open 24/7 to provide immediate care for critical conditions. We are equipped with the latest technology and staffed by skilled emergency physicians.

General Surgery

We offer a comprehensive range of surgical services, including minimally invasive procedures. Our surgeons are highly trained and use state-of-the-art equipment.

Maternity Care

Our maternity ward provides a comfortable and safe environment for expectant mothers. We offer prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care with a focus on mother and baby health.

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department specializes in the care of infants, children, and adolescents. We provide preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of conditions.

Cardiology

Our cardiology team offers advanced diagnostic and treatment options for heart-related conditions. We are committed to improving heart health and preventing cardiovascular diseases.

Diagnostic Imaging

We provide a full range of diagnostic imaging services, including X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds. Our imaging center is equipped with the latest technology for accurate diagnosis.
